Preserved from the legacy site

A history of neighbors tending shared spaces.

The former site documented volunteer-maintained boulevard plantings on Columbia Terrace and Institute, along with an alley garden program begun in 2006. Those records are useful history, not a claim that every former program or contact remains active today.

The current approach is intentionally smaller: identify an approved need, name a lead and backup, schedule one bounded workday, and invite neighbors to help.

Future coordination opportunity

Peoria’s free mulch giveaway

Peoria Public Works has hosted annual spring giveaway days for city and county residents. Dates, eligibility, loading rules, and availability must be checked each year before the neighborhood coordinates around it.
