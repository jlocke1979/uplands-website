Why neighbors give

Clear walks are a shared benefit.

01

Safer winter walking

Cleared public walks help children, older neighbors, dog walkers, students, and visitors stay out of the street.

02

Neighborhood-wide coverage

When funded, the association coordinates service across defined public sidewalks rather than private drives or steps.

03

Ready before the storm

Building the fund ahead of winter helps the association prepare before qualifying snow arrives.

The current goal, amount raised, contractor, coverage, and snowfall threshold will be published only after the association confirms the season’s arrangement.
