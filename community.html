Community

The Uplands thrives when neighbors have simple, welcoming ways to participate, care for shared places, and help one another feel at home.

Welcoming Committee

A renewed welcoming effort could help new residents feel at home without requiring a large formal committee. The first step is a simple, repeatable welcome and an invitation to the next neighborhood gathering.

  • Welcome packets for new homeowners and renters
  • Neighborhood map with key contacts and amenities
  • Introduction to URA meetings, committees, and volunteer opportunities

This initiative will move forward when a volunteer owner and backup are identified.

Block Captains

The association is considering whether a lightweight block-captain network could improve welcoming, event reminders, and two-way neighborhood communication.

  • One captain per block or street segment, as needed
  • Distributes meeting notices and urgent neighborhood updates
  • Collects feedback for board and committee consideration

This is an open conversation, not a current directory or standing commitment.
