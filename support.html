Support the Uplands

Steady, voluntary support helps the association plan ahead instead of rebuilding the budget one project at a time.

The most helpful way to give

Choose Annual Recurring Support

One automatic annual contribution gives the Uplands a more dependable base for snow planning, communications, neighborhood gatherings, beautification, and the ordinary costs of keeping a volunteer association running.

Before completing payment, please confirm the amount and billing frequency shown by PayPal. Recurring contributions can be managed or canceled through PayPal.

Payments are handled by PayPal. The Uplands website does not collect or store payment information.

One-Time Contribution

One-time contributions can be used for Annual Membership, Snow Fund, Picnic Fund, Beautification Fund, or general association support. Note which fund you are supporting in the PayPal message field.

Payments are handled by PayPal. The Uplands website does not collect or store payment information.
